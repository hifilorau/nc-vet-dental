VDCNC located at Westside Animal Hospital in Durham, NC and was founded by Don Hoover, DVM in 2015. VDCNC was established to focus on providing advanced dental services and oral surgery to dogs and cats across North Carolina. Dr. Hoover has over 15 years of experience in advanced dental procedures. In 1998, Dr Hoover became one of the first practitioners in North Carolina to add dental radiography as a part of their routine dental procedures. Since then, Dr. Hoover and his staff have completed over 4,000 dental procedures on dogs and cats.
